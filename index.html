About us

Welcome to SweetShop.com, the world's largest sweets and bakery shop! Our journey began with a passion for creating delightful and delectable sweets that bring joy to every occasion. At SweetShop.com, we believe that sweets are not just a treat but a way to share love and happiness with family and friends.

Founded in 1990 in the heart of Bhaskar Nagar, Kalwa (E), Thane, SweetShop.com started as a small family-owned business with a mission to deliver high-quality sweets made with the finest ingredients. Over the years, our dedication to excellence and our commitment to customer satisfaction have helped us grow into a renowned name in the sweets and bakery industry.

At SweetShop.com, we offer a wide range of traditional and contemporary sweets, each crafted with care and precision.


- Abdul Kareem Khan
